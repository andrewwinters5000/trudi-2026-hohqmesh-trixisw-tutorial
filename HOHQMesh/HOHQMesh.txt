Hands-On HOHQMesh

David A. Kopriva
Florida State University and
San Diego State University

and

Andrew R. Winters
Linköping University

Participants in this Hands-On tutorial will learn to generate meshes for Trixi (and other applications) using the stand-alone package HOHQMesh and its Julia front end HOHQMesh.jl. The tutorial will cover the structure of the model, which is used to define the geometry be meshed, how the HOHQMesh meshes the model, and the formats of the mesh files that are generated. These are all defined in a control file that guides the meshing process. Workflow tools HOHQMesh.jl and svg-to-HOHQMesh are available to enable visual generation of a model, mesh, and control file. The tutorial will cover installation and usage of the HOHQMesh.jl through examples that illustrate the capabilities of the application. Attendees of the tutorial are invited to bring their own laptops and to follow along and experiment with HOHQMesh, and are encouraged to bring their own geometries to model and mesh.
